Web Design Homework. Relationships between Weather and Latitude

This goal of this project is to illustrate the relationship between global weather and latitude. Depending on a cities latitude, their climate and precipitation changes. To illustrate this, we used a weather API to retrieve the data, and then utilized matplotlib to bring that data to life.
